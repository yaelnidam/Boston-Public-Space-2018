Castle Island

Image credit: Boston Cityside Realty

Castle Island is home to the 19th Century Fort Independence and covers 22 acres on the edge of Boston Harbor. Although immediately surrounded by unpopulated census tracts, the neighborhood directly west of Castle Island (South Boston) has a median income of $71,738-$100,000 (source: Boston Planning & development agency). The neighborhood to the west of Castle Island is denser than those at the southern end of the Southwest Corridor Park, with an average density at around 51-100 persons per acre (source: Boston Planning & development agency).
